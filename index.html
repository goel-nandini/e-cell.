Introduction

What is E-cell?

The Entrepreneurship Cell of Abes-ec is a student-run organisation of spirited individuals who are striving to create, foster and promote entrepreneurship among the students of Abes engineering college. The E-Cell pilots the budding entrepreneurs towards their goals through all the necessary resources like mentoring, consultancy and networking with Alumni, Entrepreneurs, Investors.

Initiatives

E-club

The E-cell community of Abes-ec provide a conduit by which members can access entrepreneurial resources. OUr aim for the coming years is to provide them with the best services.

Ecell
E-Network

E-Network is a network for E-Cells from various colleges and universities. E-network’s essential purpose is to provide mentorship to newer E-Cells and establish a network with E-Cells across India. It is currently active in the form of a slack workspace comprising of members from 35+ E-Cells from various institutions.

Ecell
Delta

Delta IIT Roorkee is an opportunity portal, currently in running its pilot phase as a Facebook Community. The community aims to connect the students from various institutes to professionals in the outside world. Delta regularly updates students with new freelance projects, internships/jobs, upcoming competitions, and more.
